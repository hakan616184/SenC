      <h2>Categories of Data Processed</h2>
      <ul>                                                                                                                                                                                         
        <li><strong>User-Provided Data:</strong> Profile info (username, age, gender), uploaded images and videos, voting preferences (swipes), and Direct Messages (DM).</li>
        <li><strong>Automatically Collected Data:</strong> Device identifiers, IP address, operating system version, app version, and app interaction statistics (e.g., session duration, feature

        <li><strong>Location Data:</strong> We do <strong>not</strong> track your GPS location in the background. Location data is only processed if you voluntarily tag a place in the "Places"   
  category via our API integration.</li>                                                                                                                                                           
        <li><strong>Authentication Data:</strong> Email address or Sign in with Apple / Google token used solely for account authentication. Passwords are never stored in plain text.</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>AI Moderation and Data Security</h2>                                                                                                                                                     
      <p>All User-Generated Content (UGC) is scanned in real-time by AI (Gemini AI) solely to detect illegal content such as nudity, violence, hate speech, or doxxing. This processing is carried

        <li><strong>Active Feed:</strong> Posts remain in the voting feed for a duration based on your subscription tier.</li>
        <li><strong>Archive:</strong> After the voting period ends, images are moved to an encrypted private archive visible only to you.</li>                                                     
        <li><strong>Inactive Accounts:</strong> Personal data associated with accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be anonymized or deleted.</li>                                         
        <li><strong>Account Deletion:</strong> You can use the <strong>"Delete Account"</strong> button within the app to submit a permanent deletion request. All personal data is irreversibly
